Our Story

Discover the passion and craftsmanship behind SierraLuxe Scents

Crafting Memories Through Fragrance

SierraLuxe Scents was born from a passion for exceptional fragrances and a desire to create scents that evoke emotions and memories.

Founded in 2010, our journey began with a simple belief: that fragrance is more than just a scentâ€”it's an experience, a memory, a statement. Our founder, Elena Sierra, traveled the world in search of the finest ingredients and traditional perfume-making techniques, bringing them together to create our signature collections.

Today, SierraLuxe Scents stands as a testament to quality, creativity, and the artistry of perfumery. Each fragrance is carefully crafted in our atelier, where modern innovation meets time-honored traditions.

Our Journey

  • 2010

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Elena Sierra founded SierraLuxe Scents with a small collection of handcrafted perfumes in a boutique studio in Sierra Leone.

  • 2013

    Expansion and Innovation

    We opened our first flagship store and expanded our collection to include luxury home fragrances and scented candles.

  • 2016

    International Recognition

    Our signature fragrance "Midnight Rose" won the prestigious International Perfume Award, putting SierraLuxe on the global map.

  • 2019

    Global Expansion

    SierraLuxe Scents expanded to international markets with boutiques in major cities around the world and an enhanced online presence.

  • 2022

    Sustainable Luxury

    We launched our eco-friendly collection, combining luxury with sustainability, and committed to environmentally responsible practices.

Our Values

Quality
